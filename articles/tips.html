Tips

Here is list of tips and tricks to avoiding malicious software and websites

General

Make sure that the website you are visiting is trustworthy before downloading anything or creating an account.
Before logging in or sending personal data make sure your connection is encrypted ("https" in URL).
Ask your parents before making any account
Make your username NOT about your personal info (gender, age, names, especially your location)
When downloading or clicking a link, think before clicking (hover over link to see if it is a fake link)
Donâ€™t open files that are sent to your email by an unknown person or open spam email
Never click advertisements
Don't post pictures of yourself on the internet or public
Don't leave your account logged into a public computer
Never talk/friend/give any information away to strangers on the internet
Install an antivirus program
Use a pop-up and ad blocker with your browser.
Keep your computer updated
Backup important files on a regular basis in case of corruption or hack.

Passwords

Do NOT share your password with others.
Make your password unique to your life and not something that is easily guessed.
Have a different password for each online account.
Write down your password and store it in a safe place away from your computer.
Change your password several times a year.
Don't use repeated characters.
Don't use keyboard patterns ("qwerty", "12345")
Don't use personal information(name, birth date, etc)
Use two-step verification

Email

Hover over From to make sure sender is legitimate.
Are the URLs legitimate? (hover over links to see hidden URL)
Look for incorrect grammar/spelling in spawm/phishing.
Look for plain text/absence of logos
Is the message body is an image
Does it request for personal information?
Does it have suspicious attachments?
Is it urgent/too good to be true?
Is my email address listed as the From address?
